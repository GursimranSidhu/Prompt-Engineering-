📄 Website Summary
============================================================

This webpage provides resources for learning and practicing various technical skills.  It offers courses, tutorials, and practice problems in areas like:

*   **Programming Languages:** C, C++, Java, Python, JavaScript, PHP, GoLang, R.
*   **Data Science & Machine Learning:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, NLP, Deep Learning.
*   **Web Development:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, ReactJS, NextJS, NodeJs, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, Django.
*   **DSA (Data Structures and Algorithms):** Problems, roadmaps, interview questions.
*   **DevOps & Cloud:** Git, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, Azure, GCP.
*   **Databases:** SQL, MySQL, PostgreSQL, PL/SQL, MongoDB.
*   **System Design:** High-level, Low-level, UML, Bootcamps.
*   **Computer Science Fundamentals:** Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Database Management, Software Engineering, Digital Logic Design, Engineering Maths.
*   **School Subjects:** Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Social Science, English Grammar.

The site also includes interview preparation materials, company-specific recruitment information, aptitude practice, job postings ("Jobs", "Job-A-Thon"), information about GfG Premium (paid service), GfG School, company information (About Us, Legal, Careers, etc.) and GATE exam preparation resources. Finally, it offers certification courses from IBM, and courses focused on placement preparation in DSA, Web Development, Data Science, and Programming Languages.
